# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free environment for all participants.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus critique on ideas, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Accept and give feedback professionally.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe environment.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to: `jason@missionmindedmakers.com`
